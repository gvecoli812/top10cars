Best Cars for High Schoolers!

As a student myself, I had trouble finding my first car. I couldn't find cars that were necessarily reliable and suited what I wanted in my car.

So I decided to create a page based on the best cars that a student can afford that may suite their wants and still be necessarily reliable! I hope this helps students all around! Note, some of the vehicles will not be the cheapest but still can be bought if you finance the vehicle.
